Welcome to Easy Recycling

Introducing our web-based Decision Support System (DSS) platform

This platform has been developed to fill the knowledge gap and achieve overall optimized industrial eco-system with high recycling rates and emission avoidance.

Among various functions, this platform serves as a one-stop knowledge repository in which industrial participants can share and discover relevant recycling technology information, waste material conversion pathways, and find trading partners to make waste-to-resource exchange transactions, providing reliable decision support in different levels.
